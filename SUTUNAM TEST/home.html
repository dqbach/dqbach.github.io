About Hotels G

Hotels G is home to savvy nomads keen on discovering the world by staying in design yet thrifty hotels with strong ties to the local creative neighborhood.

Hotels G is the lifestyle hotel brand of GCP Hospitality. A dynamic, ‘one stop’ hospitality solution with regional offices in Bangkok, Hong Kong, Myanmar and Beijing operates and develops hotels both in Asia and globally, with a portfolio of 19 hotels in major resort destinations and international business hubs.

Featured in

Hotels G have a fast-growing reputation for successfully launching and operating designer & lifestyle hotels with cutting-edge dinning outlets, creative MICE facilities and high-end serviced apartments.
